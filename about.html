About STAR

STAR (Scrub Typhus Awareness & Risk Prediction) is a comprehensive platform designed to raise awareness, provide information, and help track Scrub Typhus cases effectively. Our mission is to empower individuals, communities, and healthcare professionals with accurate and real-time data to prevent the spread of Scrub Typhus.

Scrub Typhus is a serious infectious disease caused by the Orientia tsutsugamushi bacteria. It is transmitted to humans through chigger bites, often found in areas with dense vegetation. Early detection and preventive measures are crucial, and STAR provides tools to do exactly that.

The STAR platform offers the following key features:

  • Nearby Case Tracker: Visualize current Scrub Typhus hotspots using an interactive map.
  • Online Assessment: Take a self-assessment to evaluate your risk based on symptoms and exposure.
  • Prediction System: Forecast potential outbreaks in your area using climate and historical data.
  • Contact & Support: Find nearby hospitals and communicate with healthcare professionals quickly.

STAR is fully mobile-friendly, ensuring accessibility on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Our goal is to combine awareness, technology, and actionable data to help reduce the impact of Scrub Typhus globally.
