Frequently Asked Questions

What is Scrub Typhus?

Scrub Typhus is caused by the bacteria Orientia tsutsugamushi, transmitted through infected chigger mites.

How does it spread?

It spreads through bites of infected chiggers. It does NOT spread from person to person.

What are the symptoms?

Fever, rash, headache, body ache, and sometimes stomach issues.

How can I prevent Scrub Typhus?

Wear covered clothes, use insect repellent, avoid bushy areas, and check for mites after outdoor activities.
